HTML Example - Multilayer Zoom

This example shows how the visibility of PinchZoomer contents are toggled based on the zoom level. A label is added to show the current image size that is being shown. This setup is best for scenarios where you want to show different levels of detail depending on the zoom level or sometimes screen size. This ensures that the content will look less pixelated or blurred.
